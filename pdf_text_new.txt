Rome Awaits – A Grand Beginning to Your European Journey 
Welcome to Rome, a city where ancient history, timeless architecture, and vibrant culture come 
together. Upon your arrival at the airport, our friendly representative will greet you at the arrival

setting the perfect tone for your European adventure. Get ready to explore historic wonders,

Day2 
Rome City Tour with Vatican City and St. Peter’s Basilica 
After a hearty breakfast at the hotel, the day begins with an immersive exploration of one of the 
world’s most iconic capitals—Rome, steeped in history, art, and imperial grandeur. Accompanied 
by a tour manager, the first stop is Vatican City, the smallest independent state in the world. Here, 
you'll visit the majestic St. Peter’s Basilica, a masterpiece of Renaissance architecture adorned 

=== PAGE 3 ===
with incredible works by Michelangelo and Bernini. Marvel at the grandeur of its dome, 
sculptures, and the awe-inspiring Pietà. Next, return to the heart of Rome for a panoramic city 
tour. Capture postcard-perfect photos at the Colosseum (exterior), once a grand arena of 
gladiatorial combat. The drive continues past historical landmarks like Piazza Venezia, home to 
the grand Victor Emmanuel II Monument, and the legendary Trevi Fountain, where tossing a coin 
is said to ensure your return to Rome. After soaking in the essence of Rome, regroup for a 
delicious Indian lunch. The rest of the afternoon allows time to explore or relax before returning 
to the hotel. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Indian dinner with your fellow travellers, recounting 
the highlights of your Roman adventure. (Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner) less

Day3 
Visit Leaning Tower of Pisa – Florence City Tour & Piazzale Michelangelo 
 
Start your day with a scenic drive through the Tuscan countryside as you journey from Rome 
toward the iconic city of Pisa. Upon arrival, you’ll visit the stunning Piazza dei Miracoli, or Square 

=== PAGE 4 ===
of Miracles—a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Here, marvel at the world-famous Leaning Tower of 
Pisa, a symbol of architectural wonder and human error turned tourist magnet. Surrounding this 
area are equally impressive sights like the Baptistery, Monumental Cemetery, and Cathedral of St. 
Mary of the Assumption, showcasing the Romanesque elegance of medieval Italy. After photo 
opportunities and time to appreciate Pisa's historical treasures, continue toward Florence, the 
cradle of the Renaissance. As you approach the city, your first stop is the famous Piazzale 
Michelangelo, offering a panoramic view over Florence’s red-tiled rooftops, bridges, and 
monuments—a breathtaking sight perfect for photos. During the orientation tour of Florence, 
stroll past the city’s key landmarks: the Florence Duomo, crowned by Brunelleschi’s massive 
dome, the Baptistery with its golden “Gates of Paradise”, Giotto’s Campanile, and the lively Piazza 
della Signoria, surrounded by Renaissance sculptures and the imposing Palazzo Vecchio. You’ll 
also see the charming Ponte Vecchio, a medieval stone bridge lined with artisan shops. The tour 
balances history with leisure, giving you a taste of Florence’s rich cultural heritage and its relaxed 
Tuscan charm. As the sun sets, proceed to your hotel in Bologna, where a hot Indian dinner 
awaits—perfect after a day immersed in art, architecture, and Italian legacy. (Breakfast and 
Dinner)

Day4 
Transfer To Venice – The Floating City Of Italy With Gondola Ride 
Today’s journey transports you to the magical world of Venice, a city built entirely on water, rich 
with romantic charm, artistic heritage, and timeless architecture. After breakfast, check out and 

=== PAGE 5 ===
drive to the Venetian pier, where a private boat awaits to take you across the shimmering waters 
of the Giudecca Canal. Feel the charm unfold as you arrive at the vibrant St. Mark’s Square, the 
heart of Venice. Here, admire the magnificent St. Mark’s Basilica, a masterpiece of Byzantine 
architecture adorned with intricate mosaics and golden domes. Nearby, witness the Clock Tower, 
the Bell Tower, and the famous Bridge of Sighs, connecting the Doge’s Palace to the historical 
prison complex. The view, the stories, and the atmosphere transport you to a different era. No trip 
to Venice is complete without a romantic Gondola ride. Glide through the city's narrow canals, 
flanked by grand palazzos and picturesque bridges. The peaceful sway of the boat and the 
reflections of centuries-old buildings in the water create a dreamlike experience unique to 
Venice. After this unforgettable experience, enjoy a delectable Indian lunch before continuing the 
journey towards Milan. Upon arrival, you’ll check into your hotel and unwind after a magical day. 
Venice’s elegance, serenity, and cinematic beauty are sure to remain one of the most cherished 
highlights of your European journey. (Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner)

Day5 
Discover Milan’s Elegance & Experience the Serenity of Lake Como – Drive to Zurich 
 

=== PAGE 6 ===
Begin your morning with a delicious breakfast before checking out of your hotel in Milan. Your 
first experience today is a brief yet enriching orientation tour of Milan, Italy’s fashion and design 
capital. Visit the majestic Milan Cathedral (Duomo di Milano), one of the largest and most 
intricate Gothic cathedrals in the world. Just a few steps away is the historic Galleria Vittorio 
Emanuele II, a stunning 19th-century shopping arcade with beautiful glass domes, mosaics, and 
high-end boutiques. This elegant space is also a hotspot for enjoying Italian coffee and capturing 
some stunning photographs. After exploring Milan, you’ll travel north to the breathtakingly scenic 
region of Lake Como, a favourite getaway for celebrities and romantics alike. Nestled against the 
Alpine foothills, this lake is famed for its dramatic landscape, sparkling waters, and elegant 
lakeside villas. Visit the charming village of Bellagio (subject to road/weather conditions), often 
referred to as the “Pearl of Lake Como.” Here, you can stroll through narrow cobbled streets, 
admire pastel-colored buildings, and soak in views of the serene lakefront. Following this 
picturesque escape, continue your journey across the Swiss border into Zurich, Switzerland’s 
largest city. Relax in your comfortable coach and enjoy the changing landscapes as the terrain 
shifts from Italian elegance to Swiss precision and alpine charm. Upon arrival in Zurich by 
evening, settle into your hotel and enjoy a hearty Indian dinner. Today beautifully blends urban 
style, natural beauty, and cross-border adventure—offering a taste of Italy’s vibrant cities and the 
tranquil elegance of Switzerland’s countryside. (Breakfast and Dinner)

After breakfast, get ready for a thrilling alpine experience as we head to the mountain village of 
Engelberg. From here, enjoy a series of cable car rides leading up to the spectacular Mt. Titlis,

and travel to the beautiful city of Lucerne. Enjoy an orientation tour of its highlights, including the 
Chapel Bridge (Kapellbrücke). Enjoy some free time to shop for Swiss souvenirs like watches, 
chocolates, and knives before heading back to Zurich. (Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner) less

Day7 
Magical Alpine Excursion To The Top Of Europe – The Amazing Jungfraujoch And 
Scenic Interlaken  
 

=== PAGE 8 ===
After breakfast, get ready for a truly magical Alpine experience. Today, you have the opportunity 
to join an unforgettable excursion to Jungfraujoch – The Top of Europe (available at an 
additional cost). We begin by heading to Grindelwald Terminal, where the state-of-the-art Eiger 
Express gondola swiftly takes you to Eigergletscher Station in just 15 minutes. From there, board 
the legendary cogwheel train that ascends to the highest railway station in Europe at 11,333 feet. 
At the summit, immerse yourself in a world of eternal snow and ice. Wander through the Ice 
Palace, where artists sculpt masterpieces in frozen form, and take in jaw-dropping views of the 
Aletsch Glacier from the panoramic Sphinx Observatory — a true high point of your European 
adventure. Those who prefer a more relaxed day can spend time in Interlaken, a scenic town 
nestled between two lakes and surrounded by the majestic Swiss Alps. Whether shopping for 
Swiss souvenirs or enjoying a lakeside walk, Interlaken offers a peaceful escape. Later in the day, 
regroup and return to Zurich for the night. (Breakfast and Dinner)

Day4 
From the Roar of Rhine Falls to the Romance of Paris 
 
After breakfast, check out and begin your day with a stop at one of Switzerland’s most 
captivating natural wonders – the Rhine Falls in Schaffhausen. As Europe’s largest waterfall, the 
Rhine Falls offers a mesmerizing sight as water thunders down a 150-meter-wide drop. Take in 
panoramic views from the observation decks, capturing unforgettable moments against a 
backdrop of mist and power. It’s the perfect way to bid farewell to Switzerland’s natural beauty. 
From here, relax in the comfort of your luxury coach as you journey across the Swiss-French 
border toward Paris, the world-renowned City of Lights. The long but scenic drive offers changing 
vistas – from serene Alpine valleys to charming French countryside – providing a smooth 
transition from nature’s raw grandeur to urban elegance. Upon arrival in Paris by early evening, 
complete the hotel check-in and take some time to relax. The evening is yours to explore at

discussing the journey so far and looking ahead to Paris’ iconic attractions awaiting you in the
