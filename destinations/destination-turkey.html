Overview

Turkey is two countries stitched into one trip — Istanbul's centuries of layered history, and Cappadocia's otherworldly valleys, best seen from a hundred metres up in a hot air balloon at sunrise.

Ten days lets you do both properly, plus a coastal close to the trip that most itineraries skip entirely.

  • Sunrise hot air balloon flight over Cappadocia
  • Hagia Sophia & Blue Mosque, Istanbul
  • Bosphorus ferry crossing
  • Derinkuyu underground city
  • Cave-hotel overnight stay
  • Aegean or Mediterranean coastal finish

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1–3

Istanbul

Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, Bosphorus ferry, spice bazaar and a free day to explore a neighbourhood of your choice.

Day 4

Fly to Cappadocia

Short flight to Nevşehir, afternoon at the Göreme Open Air Museum.

Day 5

Balloon Sunrise

The 5am pickup and hour-long flight that this whole trip is built around.

Day 6

Underground & Valleys

Derinkuyu underground city and a valley hike between Göreme and Çavuşin.

Day 7–9

Coastal Close

Fly to Antalya or Bodrum for a slower final stretch on the water.

Day 10

Departure

Fly home from the coast or back via Istanbul.
