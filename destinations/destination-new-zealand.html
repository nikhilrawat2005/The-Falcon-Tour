Two islands, one unforgettable loop.

Overview

New Zealand rewards travellers who don't try to do everything. We weight this itinerary toward the South Island's concentrated landscapes, with enough North Island time to round out the trip.

Twelve days, real distances respected, no day lost entirely to driving.

  • Milford Sound fjord cruise
  • Kawarau Bridge bungee jump
  • Shotover River jet boat ride
  • Lake Pukaki & Aoraki/Mount Cook
  • Rotorua geothermal parks
  • Auckland harbour finale

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1–3

Queenstown

Bungee jumping, jet boating, cable car views over the lake.

Day 4

Milford Sound

Full-day fjord cruise, one of the most photographed spots on earth.

Day 5–6

Wanaka & Mount Cook

Lake Pukaki's blue waters and a glacier valley walk.

Day 7–8

South Island Transit

Scenic drive north, one-lane bridges and all.

Day 9–10

Rotorua

Geothermal parks, Māori cultural experiences.

Day 11–12

Auckland & Departure

Harbour walks, Sky Tower, flight home.
