Overview

Sri Lanka fits a disproportionate amount of country into one week — hill-country tea estates, a colonial fort town, a wildlife safari, and a south coast that swings between surf towns and quiet coves.

We route inland first, then let the island unwind toward the coast, so the trip builds rather than backtracks.

  • Kandy–Ella scenic train ride
  • Little Adam's Peak sunrise hike
  • Nine Arch Bridge
  • Yala National Park safari
  • Galle Fort walking tour
  • Mirissa beach & whale watching (seasonal)

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1

Kandy

Temple of the Tooth, Peradeniya Botanical Gardens.

Day 2

Train to Ella

The Kandy–Ella railway through rolling tea estates.

Day 3

Ella

Little Adam's Peak sunrise, Nine Arch Bridge, a tea factory tour.

Day 4

Yala Safari

Morning game drive, transfer to the south coast.

Day 5–6

South Coast

Galle Fort, Unawatuna beach, Mirissa whale watching.

Day 7

Colombo & Departure

One night in the capital before the flight out.
