Scuba Diving

Descend beneath the surface and explore vibrant coral reefs and marine life up close, with certified instructors and full safety gear for every level from total beginner to advanced diver.

Sessions include a shallow-water practice run before heading to the main reef site.

Duration2–3 hrs
DifficultyBeginner–Advanced

Frequently Asked

Do I need certification?

No — beginner sessions include full training. Certified divers can go deeper and longer.

Can non-swimmers try it?

Yes, with flotation support and a shallow introductory dive first.

What's included?

Full gear, instructor, boat transfer and a safety briefing.
