Skyline Luge

Ride downhill on a gravity-powered luge cart, controlling your own speed the whole way down — genuinely fun and safe for kids and adults alike.

A chairlift takes you back up for as many runs as your session allows, across multiple track routes.

Duration30–40 min
DifficultyEasy
Min Age6+
Starting From₹1,800 / person

Frequently Asked

Do I control the speed?

Yes, a hand-operated brake gives you full control throughout.

Is it safe for kids?

Yes, from age 6 with an accompanying adult on tandem karts.

How many rides are included?

Sessions typically include 2–3 runs; extra rides can be added.
