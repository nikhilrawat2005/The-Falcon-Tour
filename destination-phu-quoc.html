Overview

Phu Quoc is the trip we recommend when the ask is simply 'somewhere quiet.' Five days, barely any itinerary, and a coastline that rewards doing very little.

The only structure worth keeping is the cable car and a snorkel trip — everything else can stay open.

  • Hon Thom cable car — longest three-rope sea crossing
  • An Thoi archipelago snorkelling
  • Duong Dong night market
  • Fish sauce factory visit
  • West-coast sunset beaches
  • Zero fixed-plan afternoons

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival

Check in, beach walk, dinner at a local seafood shack.

Day 2

Cable Car & Sun World

Hon Thom crossing and a beach afternoon.

Day 3

Snorkelling

Boat trip through the An Thoi archipelago.

Day 4

Island Culture

Fish sauce factory, Duong Dong night market.

Day 5

Departure

A last sunset at Long Beach before the flight home.
