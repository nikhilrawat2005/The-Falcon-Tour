Overview

Azerbaijan is our most underrated route — Baku's old town against a skyline of flame-shaped towers, mud volcanoes that genuinely bubble cold mud, and mountain towns most travellers haven't heard of yet.

Six days is enough to cover Baku properly and still get into the Caucasus foothills.

  • Flame Towers light show
  • İçəri Şəhər (Old City) & Maiden Tower
  • Gobustan mud volcanoes & petroglyphs
  • Sheki's stained-glass khan's palace
  • Gabala cable car
  • Caspian Sea boardwalk sunset

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1–2

Baku

Old City, Maiden Tower, Flame Towers at sunset.

Day 3

Gobustan

Mud volcanoes and ancient rock petroglyphs, 40 minutes from Baku.

Day 4–5

Sheki & Gabala

Silk Road old town and a mountain cable car ride.

Day 6

Baku & Departure

Caspian boardwalk, last plov dinner, flight out.
