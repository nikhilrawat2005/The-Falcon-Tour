Overview

Singapore is compact enough to plan tightly and diverse enough that five days never feels repetitive — skyline, islands, food and wildlife, all a short MRT ride apart.

This is our most-booked short-haul itinerary, tuned over enough repeat trips to know exactly where the queues are worth beating.

  • Gardens by the Bay Supertree light show
  • Universal Studios Singapore
  • Skyline Luge on Sentosa
  • Cable car crossing to Sentosa Island
  • Night Safari at Singapore Zoo
  • Hawker centre food crawl

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival & Marina Bay

Gardens by the Bay at dusk, hawker dinner at Lau Pa Sat.

Day 2

Sentosa

Universal Studios, Skyline Luge, cable car crossing.

Day 3

City & Culture

Chinatown, Little India, Orchard Road shopping.

Day 4

Wildlife

Singapore Zoo by day, Night Safari after dark.

Day 5

Last Light & Departure

Marina Bay Sands SkyPark, last-minute shopping, evening flight.
