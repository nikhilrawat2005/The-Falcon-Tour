Overview

Malaysia splits cleanly between Kuala Lumpur's skyline energy and Langkawi's island quiet — a short flight apart, and different enough that six days never feels like one long trip.

We build in real do-nothing time on the islands, on purpose.

  • Petronas Towers skybridge
  • Batu Caves at sunrise
  • Jalan Alor night food street
  • Langkawi SkyBridge & cable car
  • Island-hopping snorkel trip
  • Duty-free shopping stop
