Day-by-Day Itinerary

Cross from the North Island's volcanic plateaus and fictional Shire villages to the dramatic fiords and adventure hotspots of the South Island.

01
Arrival in Auckland

Welcome to the City of Sails

Arrive at Auckland Airport. Receive a private transfer to your hotel. In the afternoon, visit the famous Sky Tower for a 360-degree view of Auckland's harbors. Conclude your day with a welcome dinner overlooking the yachts in Viaduct Harbour.

Airport Private Transfer Sky Tower Admission Viaduct Harbour Welcome Dinner
02
Hobbiton Movie Set

Journey into Middle-earth

Depart Auckland and drive to Matamata. Step into the lush pastures of the Shire on a guided walking tour of the Hobbiton Movie Set, featured in the Lord of the Rings films. See hobbit holes, the mill, and enjoy a complimentary beverage at the Green Dragon Inn.

Hobbiton Guided Tour Green Dragon Inn Drink Matamata Scenic Drive
03
Rotorua Geothermal

Boiling Mud Pools & Maori Culture

Explore the geothermal wonders of Wai-O-Tapu Thermal Wonderland, famous for its colorful champagne pools and erupting geysers. In the evening, visit Te Puia for a traditional Maori cultural performance followed by a hot-stone Hangi feast.

Wai-O-Tapu Geothermal Entry Te Puia Cultural Show Traditional Hangi Feast
04
Fly to Queenstown

Arrival in the Adventure Capital

Take a morning flight from Auckland to Queenstown on the South Island. Ride the Skyline Gondola to Bob's Peak for panoramic views of Lake Wakatipu and the Remarkables. Have fun riding the luge tracks before enjoying a lakeside evening walk.

Domestic Flight to Queenstown Skyline Gondola Ride Bob's Peak Luge Tracks
05
Queenstown Adventure

Free Day for Thrills & Wine

A free day to choose your adventure. Opt for a thrilling jet boat ride down the Shotover River canyon, a historic bungy jump off Kawarau Bridge, skydiving, or enjoy a relaxed tour of the famous Central Otago boutique wineries.

Bungy / Jetboat Options Otago Wine Tasting Option Lake Wakatipu Views
06
Milford Sound Excursion

Cruise the Eighth Wonder of the World

Drive through the majestic Fiordland National Park along the Milford Road. Board an afternoon wilderness cruise on Milford Sound. Glide past towering waterfalls, Mitre Peak, and look for local seals, dolphins, and penguins.

Milford Sound Wilderness Cruise Fiordland National Park Drive Mitre Peak Photography
07
Queenstown to Christchurch

Crossing the Southern Alps

Depart Queenstown and drive north through the dramatic Southern Alps. Pass beautiful glacial lakes like Tekapo and Pukaki. Arrive in the historic English-influenced city of Christchurch and visit the lush Christchurch Botanic Gardens.

Southern Alps Scenic Drive Lake Tekapo & Pukaki Stops Christchurch Botanic Gardens
08
Departure

Farewell New Zealand

Enjoy your final breakfast in Christchurch. Check out of your hotel and transfer to Christchurch International Airport for your flight home.

Airport Departure Transfer Breakfast Included
