Leisure Day in Venice

Today spend a leisure day in Venice at your own pace — a city where every corner tells a story. DAY 6

Mount Pilatus Tour

In the morning, gear up for an unforgettable Mount Pilatus adventure. Using your Swiss Pass, reach the Lucerne bus station and board a bus to Kriens — the gateway to the mountain. From here, ascend via a gondola ride that offers stunning views of the Swiss countryside. At the summit, enjoy thrilling experiences like the famous toboggan run, the longest in Switzerland, and take in stunning views of 73 Alpine peaks stretching across the horizon. Afterwards, return to the hotel using a swiss pass. Lucerne Walking Tour with Lake Lucerne Cruise

Begin your morning with a walking tour through the heart of Lucerne, a city where history and charm meet at every corner. Start with a visit to the iconic Chapel Bridge, one of Europe’s oldest covered wooden bridges, adorned with 17th-century paintings. Continue to the Lion Monument, a poignant rock carving that pays tribute to the Swiss Guards who lost their lives during the French Revolution. Later, enjoy a cruise on Lake Lucerne, where the calm waters reflect the surrounding Swiss Alps. Activity Lucerne Walking Tour with Lake Lucerne Cruise You’ll be covering these amazing experiences 1.
