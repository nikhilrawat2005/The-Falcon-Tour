Day-by-Day Itinerary

Discover the premium experiences curated by The Falcon Tour. Balanced pacing, local guides, and top class stays.

01
Day 1

Arrival in Stockholm

Welcome to Sweden! Upon arrival at Stockholm Airport, take your included transfer to the hotel. Spend the rest of the day relaxing or starting to explore the capital's beautiful islands at your own pace. Detailed tour info will be at the reception.

ArrivalLeisure Day
02
Day 2

Stockholm – Mora

Enjoy a guided panoramic city tour of Stockholm. Explore Gamla Stan, the medieval heart of the city, which is home to the majestic Royal Palace, Stockholm Cathedral, the Nobel Museum, and colorful historic squares. Stop at the Fjällgatan viewpoint for amazing photo opportunities. In the afternoon, drive through scenic Swedish forests and lake districts to Mora, arriving in the evening.

Stockholm SightsGamla Stan TourMora Stay
03
Day 3

Mora – Funäsdalen – Røros – Trondheim

Depart early from Sweden toward the Norwegian border, traveling past beautiful lakes, forests, and alpine landscapes. Stop at Funäsdalen, a popular alpine ski resort. Cross into Norway and stop at the UNESCO World Heritage town of Røros, a uniquely preserved 17th-century copper mining town. Continue to Trondheim, Norway's historic Viking-era capital.

Border CrossingRøros UNESCO SiteViking Capital
04
Day 4

Trondheim – Kristiansund – Atlantic Road – Molde

Travel along Norway's stunning coastline with scenic ferry crossings. Stop in Kristiansund, a charming island city built across four islands, with leisure time for lunch. Visit the beautifully preserved Kvernes Stave Church (entrance included). Experience the world-famous Atlantic Road, driving over its dramatic bridges stretching across islets and the open ocean. Arrive in Molde, the town of roses.

Kvernes Stave ChurchAtlantic Road DriveScenic Bridges
05
Day 5

Molde – Geiranger – Briksdalsbreen – Loen

A spectacular day through world-famous Norwegian fjords and glaciers. Take a ferry crossing of the Romsdal Fjord, followed by dramatic mountain drives. Stop at the Eagle Viewpoint for breathtaking views over the UNESCO-listed Geiranger Fjord. Visit the spectacular Briksdalsbreen Glacier. Continue to the scenic village of Loen. Dinner is included tonight.

Geiranger FjordBriksdalsbreen GlacierIncluded Dinner
06
Day 6

Loen – Bergen

Take a scenic drive to Bergen, passing fjord inlets, mountains, and taking ferry crossings. Upon arrival in Bergen, ride the Fløibanen funicular up to the peak of Mount Fløyen for spectacular panoramic views of the city. Spend the afternoon exploring the UNESCO-listed Bryggen Wharf, the historic fish market, and local harborside cafes.

Funicular to Mt. FløyenBryggen WharfBergen Arrival
07
Day 7

Bergen – Eidfjord – Vøringsfossen – Geilo – Oslo

Depart Bergen and drive over the impressive Hardangerfjord suspension bridge. Stop in the beautiful fjord town of Eidfjord and visit the powerful, plunging Vøringsfossen Waterfall. Drive across the high mountain plateaus of Hardangervidda to Geilo. Continue to Oslo, arriving by evening.

Vøringsfossen WaterfallHardanger BridgePlateau Drive
08
Day 8

Oslo City Tour – Overnight Ferry to Copenhagen

Enjoy a guided panoramic tour of Oslo, covering Frogner Park (Vigeland sculptures), the Royal Palace, Town Hall, Oslo Opera House, and the bustling harbor. Enjoy free time before boarding a modern overnight ferry to Copenhagen, Denmark. Enjoy cabin accommodation and a relaxing sea cruise.

Oslo City TourOvernight FerryScenic Cruise
09
Day 9

Arrival in Copenhagen – City Tour

Arrive in Copenhagen and embark on a guided city tour. Visit the iconic Little Mermaid statue, stroll along the colorful Nyhavn harbor canals, view the exterior of Amalienborg Palace, and see the Tivoli area. The afternoon is free to shop, dine, and explore at your leisure.

Copenhagen SightsLittle MermaidNyhavn Walk
10
Day 10

Departure from Copenhagen

Enjoy breakfast at the hotel. Checkout and transfer to Copenhagen Airport for your flight back home, filled with unforgettable Scandinavian memories.

BreakfastAirport Departure
