Day-by-Day Itinerary

A complete journey from Kandy's temples to the tea estates of Nuwara Eliya and the beaches of Bentota.

01
Arrival in Colombo & Transfer to Kandy

Journey to the Hill Capital

Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport, Colombo, and be welcomed by our representative. Drive to Kandy, Sri Lanka's cultural capital nestled amid green hills. In the evening, visit the sacred Temple of the Tooth. Overnight stay in Kandy.

Airport Transfer to Kandy Temple of the Tooth
02
Transfer to Nuwara Eliya

Into Sri Lanka's Tea Country

Drive up into the misty highlands towards Nuwara Eliya, known as "Little England". En route, stop at a working Tea Plantation & Factory to see how Ceylon tea is grown, plucked, and processed, with a tasting session.

Tea Plantation & Factory Visit Scenic Hill Country Drive
03
Nuwara Eliya Sightseeing

Waterfalls, Lakes & Scenic Rails

Visit the cascading Ramboda Waterfall and stroll around the picturesque Gregory Lake. Subject to availability, take in a scenic train journey through the hill country, one of the most celebrated rail routes in the world.

Ramboda Waterfall Gregory Lake Scenic Train Journey (Subject to Availability)
04
Transfer to Bentota

From the Hills to the Coast

Descend from the highlands to the coastal town of Bentota. On arrival, enjoy a Madu River Boat Safari through mangrove islands and cinnamon plantations. Overnight stay in Bentota.

Madu River Safari Coastal Descent Drive
05
Bentota Beach Day

Leisure by the Indian Ocean

A free day at leisure on Bentota Beach. Relax by the resort pool, take a stroll along the shore, or opt for water sports such as jet-skiing and banana boat rides at your own cost.

Bentota Beach Optional Water Sports
06
Departure

Farewell Sri Lanka

Enjoy a final breakfast at the resort before checking out. Transfer to Bandaranaike International Airport, Colombo, for your onward flight.

Airport Departure Transfer Breakfast Included
