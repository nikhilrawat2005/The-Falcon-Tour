Day-by-Day Itinerary

A carefully paced, fully-escorted itinerary crossing from the historic ruins of Rome to the majestic peaks of central Switzerland.

01
Arrival in Rome

Welcome to the Eternal City

Arrive at Rome airport, clear customs, and receive a private transfer to your hotel. Spend your afternoon relaxing or walking down cobblestone alleys. In the evening, gather for a brief orientation and enjoy a stroll to the famous Trevi Fountain, followed by a warm Italian welcome dinner.

Private Airport Transfer Trevi Fountain Stroll Welcome Dinner Included
02
Rome Sightseeing

Vatican Treasures & Ancient Ruins

Dive into Rome's unparalleled history. In the morning, bypass the crowds with skip-the-line tickets to the Colosseum and the Roman Forum. In the afternoon, cross the Tiber River to tour the Vatican Museums, stand inside the Sistine Chapel, and marvel at the colossal St. Peter's Basilica.

Skip-the-line Colosseum Entry Sistine Chapel Guided Tour Vatican Museums
03
Pisa & Florence

Tuscan Hills & Renaissance Wonders

Depart Rome and drive north through the rolling hills of Tuscany. Stop at Pisa to photograph the famous Leaning Tower in the Field of Miracles. Continue to Florence, the cradle of the Renaissance, where you will explore the Duomo, walk across Ponte Vecchio, and view the iconic Florence skyline.

Leaning Tower of Pisa Stop Florence City Walk Duomo & Ponte Vecchio
04
Venice

Canals & Glassblowing Artisans

Depart for the lagoon city of Venice. Board a private boat to St. Mark's Square. Explore the Basilica and Doge's Palace, followed by a glass-making demonstration by master artisans. Conclude your day with a classic gondola ride through Venice's peaceful side canals.

St. Mark's Square Boat Transfer Private Gondola Ride Glassblowing Workshop
05
Milan

Fashion, Castles & The Great Duomo

Drive west to Milan, Italy's fashion capital. Walk the magnificent Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II and stand in awe of the Duomo di Milano. Visit the historic Sforza Castle and enjoy free time to shop in the fashionable Brera district.

Duomo di Milano Visit Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II Sforza Castle
06
Zurich & Rhine Falls

Crossing into Switzerland

Cross the border into Switzerland and enjoy a scenic drive through the Swiss countryside. Arrive in Zurich for a brief city tour of the historic quarter and beautiful lakefront, followed by a visit to the roaring Rhine Falls, Europe's largest waterfall.

Swiss Alps Scenic Drive Zurich Old Town Tour Rhine Falls Entry
07
Lucerne

Mt. Pilatus Cable Car & Lake Cruise

Spend your day in Lucerne, one of Switzerland's most beautiful towns. See the historic Chapel Bridge and the Lion Monument. Take a thrilling cable car ride to the peak of Mount Pilatus for panorama views, and wind down with a relaxing cruise across Lake Lucerne.

Mt. Pilatus Cable Car Lake Lucerne Boat Cruise Chapel Bridge Walk
08
Departure

Farewell Europe

After breakfast, check out of your hotel. Enjoy a final morning of shopping in Lucerne before a private group transfer to Zurich Airport for your international return flight home.

Airport Departure Transfer Breakfast Included
