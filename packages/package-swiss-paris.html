Day-by-Day Itinerary

Experience the perfect contrast between snow-covered Swiss peaks and the romantic, high-fashion streets of Paris.

01
Arrival in Zurich

Welcome to Switzerland

Arrive at Zurich Airport and receive a transfer to your hotel. After settling in, embark on a brief city orientation tour. Discover Lake Zurich, the historic Old Town (Altstadt), and the luxury shopping district along Bahnhofstrasse.

Airport Transfer Zurich Orientation Tour Bahnhofstrasse Walk
02
Mt. Titlis Snow Adventure

Peak Snow Activities & Cliff Walk

Take a scenic drive to Engelberg. Board the famous Rotair revolving cable car to reach the peak of Mount Titlis (3,020m). Walk inside the Glacier Cave, cross the thrilling Titlis Cliff Walk (Europe's highest suspension bridge), and play in the snow parks.

Titlis Rotair Cable Car Glacier Cave Walk Cliff Suspension Bridge
03
Lucerne Exploration

Historic Bridges & Lake Cruise

Spend the day exploring Lucerne. Walk the medieval Chapel Bridge (Kapellbr�cke) and view the touching Lion Monument. In the afternoon, embark on a scenic panorama boat cruise on Lake Lucerne with the Swiss Alps framing the landscape.

Lake Lucerne Boat Cruise Chapel Bridge Walk Lion Monument Visit
04
Zurich to Paris by Train

Scenic Rail to the City of Light

Board a high-speed Eurail train to Paris. Enjoy the changing landscapes from Swiss mountains to French farmlands. Check into your hotel in Paris and spend your evening taking a scenic walk along the Seine River.

High-Speed Eurail Train Scenic Border Crossing Seine Evening Walk
05
Paris Highlights

Eiffel Tower Views & Landmarks

Explore Paris's most famous landmarks. Ascend to the second floor of the Eiffel Tower for sweeping views of the city. Drive along the Arc de Triomphe, Champs-�lys�es, and Place de la Concorde, with an exterior visit to the world-famous Louvre Museum.

Eiffel Tower Access Tickets Champs-�lys�es Tour Louvre Museum Exterior Stop
06
Disneyland Paris

A Full Day of Magic

Enjoy a full day of fun at Disneyland Paris. Experience thrilling roller coasters, walk through fairy-tale castles, meet your favorite Disney characters, and watch the spectacular evening parade and illuminations over Sleeping Beauty Castle.

Disneyland 1-Day Pass Disney Character Meet Evening Castle Fireworks
07
Departure

Adieu Paris

After breakfast, enjoy last-minute shopping at Galeries Lafayette. A private transfer will pick you up from your hotel to take you to Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG) Airport for your departure flight.

Galeries Lafayette Visit Airport Departure Transfer
