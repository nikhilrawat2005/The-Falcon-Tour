Day-by-Day Itinerary

Discover the premium experiences curated by The Falcon Tour. Balanced pacing, local guides, and top class stays.

01
Day 1

Arrival in Oslo – Gateway to the Fjords

Welcome to Norway! Upon arrival at Oslo Airport, you will be met and transferred to your premium hotel. Check in and spend the rest of the day at leisure, relaxing after your journey or taking your first walk around this beautiful harbor city.

ArrivalLeisure
02
Day 2

Glide Through Oslo’s Fjord & Holmenkollen Ski Jump

Embark on a scenic sightseeing cruise through the island-studded Oslo Fjord. Afterward, proceed to the legendary Holmenkollen Ski Jump, which offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city and fjord below. Enjoy a delicious Indian dinner in the evening.

Fjord CruiseHolmenkollen ViewpointIndian Dinner
03
Day 3

Explore Oslo & Enjoy a Scenic Drive to Stockholm

Start your morning with a guided orientation tour of Oslo's highlights. Afterward, board your comfortable coach for a highly scenic journey across the borders to Stockholm, Sweden’s regal and romantic capital. Enjoy a relaxing dinner on arrival.

City TourScenic Border CrossingIndian Dinner
04
Day 4

Stockholm City Tour & Overnight Baltic Cruise to Helsinki

Take a guided tour of Stockholm, covering the medieval lanes of Gamla Stan, the stunning Royal Palace, and locations honoring Nobel laureates. In the afternoon, board a luxurious overnight Silja Line cruise to Helsinki. Admire the breathtaking Swedish archipelago as you dine on board.

Gamla StanOvernight Baltic CruiseArchipelago Views
05
Day 5

Helsinki Highlights, Porvoo Old Town & Rock Church Visit

Disembark in Finland’s capital. Explore Helsinki’s modernist charm, including Senate Square and the unique Temppeliaukio (Rock Church). In the afternoon, take a trip to the nearby medieval town of Porvoo, famous for its historic red wooden storehouses and cobblestone streets.

Rock ChurchPorvoo Old TownHelsinki Sights
06
Day 6

Explore Tallinn & Ride the Santa Claus Express

Take a morning ferry across the Gulf of Finland to Tallinn, Estonia. Enjoy a guided walking tour of its UNESCO-listed medieval Old Town with its fairytale turrets and cobbled streets. Return to Helsinki by ferry and board the legendary Santa Claus Express overnight sleeper train bound for the Arctic Circle.

Tallinn Old TownFerry CruiseSanta Claus Express Train
07
Day 7

Arctic Wildlife at Ranua Zoo & Northern Lights Chase

Wake up in Rovaniemi, the official hometown of Santa Claus. Head to the Ranua Wildlife Zoo to observe majestic polar bears, lynx, and other Arctic species in their natural habitat. In the evening, set off on a thrilling, guided Northern Lights safari under Lapland's dark starry skies.

Ranua ZooNorthern Lights ChaseArctic Circle Entry
08
Day 8

Excursion to Santa Claus Village with Husky Farm & Safari

Visit the magical Santa Claus Village, cross the Arctic Circle line, and send a postcard from Santa's Post Office. Later, visit a local Husky Farm and enjoy an exciting, fast-paced sled ride pulled by a team of enthusiastic huskies through snowy trails.

Santa Claus VillageHusky Sled RideArctic Experience
09
Day 9

Departure

Check out from your hotel after breakfast. Transfer to Rovaniemi Airport for your flight back home, carrying magical Arctic memories.

BreakfastAirport Transfer
