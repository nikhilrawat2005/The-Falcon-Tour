Common Questions About Visiting Japan

Japan's language, customs and transport system raise plenty of first-time questions, here are answers to what travellers ask us most.

Do I Need to Speak Japanese

No, major cities have English signage, especially on trains, and translation apps handle most everyday situations. A few basic phrases go a long way and are appreciated.

Is Japan Expensive

It's more affordable than many expect, street food, convenience stores and budget accommodation keep costs reasonable, though bullet trains and hotels in central Tokyo can add up.

How Do I Get Around

Trains are the backbone of travel in Japan, punctual, extensive and easy to navigate. The Japan Rail Pass is worth it for multi-city itineraries covering long distances.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa for Japan?

Many nationalities can enter visa-free for short tourist stays, check requirements for your specific passport.

What currency does Japan use?

The Japanese Yen (JPY), and cash is still widely used alongside cards.
